LandLedger

The current land registration system faces several challenges that hinder efficiency and trust. The opaque processes, reliance on physical documents, and delays in manual verification further exacerbate these problems. Traditional methods in land registration are plagued with multiple risks and inefficiencies, leaving buyers and sellers vulnerable to fraud and delays.
These challenges include:

  • iProperty fraud, such as double selling and fake documents.
  • Manual record tampering leading to inaccuracies.
  • Data silos between departments, hindering collaboration.
  • Corruption in the registration process.
  • Title disputes and prolonged litigation due to inefficient systems.
A blockchain-based land registration system resolves these issues by creating a secure, transparent, and efficient platform. Blockchain ensures tamper-proof ownership records, preventing double registration fraud and document tampering. It provides real-time status updates, automated verification, and complete transaction histories, improving transparency and reducing corruption. Digital records stored using IPFS are cryptographically secured, eliminating risks of forgery, loss, or damage. By breaking down data silos, the system enables seamless interdepartmental coordination, accelerating processes and minimizing title disputes. This modern solution ensures trust, accuracy, and efficiency in land registration.

Askpert

Knowledge retention is a challenge for organisations across various sectors. The loss of intellectual capital when employees leave without an effective knowledge transfer leads to inefficient operations and disrupts business continuity.
The traditional model of knowledge management has created data silos within the organisations and leads:

  • Lack of collaboration between multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Inadequate mentoring for less experienced employees.
  • Non-productive time searching for information.
  • High employee training costs.
  • Data integration challenges for wholistic insights.
An AI-driven assistant with multimodal data handling capabilities was developed by integrating RAG techniques for CAE domain. The application demonstrates strong performance in handling text, images, and mathematical queries. The goal is to make sure the company never “forgets” critical information and keeps the employees informed, hence maintaining competitive advantage and improved financial metrics.
